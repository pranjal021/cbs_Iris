Send Email with Speech

Click start button & start speaking!

Instructions:

    Click Start button and click 'allow' to use microphone if popup comes up.
  1. After allowing, just start talking means your subject text.
  2. Speaking Commands:
    • For comma(,) say 'comma'.
    • For fullstop(.) say 'full stop'.
    • For exclamation(!) say 'exclamation sign'.
    • For question mark(?) say 'question mark'.
    • For delete something, say 'delete now'.
  3. After completing your subject, say 'line break' to break the line for your body content. After break, the body content part will start.
  4. Say 'paragraph break' for provide break between two lines.
  5. When everything's done means after completing your body part, say 'send email' to open your default email provider(wheather it's a app or gmail account) and send mail specifically via gmail, just say send gmail and it'll open gmail.com and then just edit your recipient's email address and send email.
  6. If you send email without providing 'line break' command (see point 4), then subject and body both will take the same content.
